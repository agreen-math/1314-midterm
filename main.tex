 \(= 4 \, h^{2} + 8 \, h x + 4 \, x^{2} - 8\) 

 \(\displaystyle{\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h} = \frac{ (4 \, h^{2} + 8 \, h x + 4 \, x^{2} - 8) - (4 \, x^{2} - 8) }{h}}\) 

 \(=\displaystyle{ \frac{ 4 \, h^{2} + 8 \, h x }{h}}\) 

 \(= \displaystyle{ \frac{ h(4 \, h + 8 \, x) }{h}}\) 

 \(\boxed{ 4 \, h + 8 \, x }\) 

}
