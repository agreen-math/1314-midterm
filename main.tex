 \(=\displaystyle{ \frac{ 6 \, h^{2} + 12 \, h x }{h}}\) 

 \(= \displaystyle{ \frac{ h(6 \, h + 12 \, x) }{h}}\) 

 \(\boxed{ 6 \, h + 12 \, x }\) 

}
